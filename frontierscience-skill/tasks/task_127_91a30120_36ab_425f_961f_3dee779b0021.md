You have data from this experiment that involves different halogen solvents: 1,2,4-trichlorobenzene (TCB) and triiodobenzene (TIB). The aim is to understand how the presence of halogens affects the breaking energy of the Au−S bond.
Question: Given:

- In TCB, there are two plateau lengths observed: 0.97 nm (without Cl interaction) and 0.59 nm (with Cl interaction).
