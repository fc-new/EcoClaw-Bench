Please observe that, so as for the vehicle to not disintegrate during the acceleration process, we enforce that, for any constituent atom of the vehicle, when viewed within the instantaneous rest frame of that atom, the relative distance between that atom and any other atom of the vehicle does not change over time.
Give your answer in terms of `\(L, a, v\)` and `\(c\)`.
