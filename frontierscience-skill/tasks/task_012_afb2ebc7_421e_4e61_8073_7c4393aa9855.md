## Prompt

`\((x,y)\colon(0,0)\to\left(0,L_{y}\right)\to\left(L_{x},L_{y}\right)\to\left(L_{x},0\right)\to(0,0)\)`

Assuming `\(m \ll M\)` and `\(L_x, L_y \ll R\)`, in the calculations, you only need to take the non-zero lowest order terms of `\(m/M\)`, `\(L_x/R\)`, and `\(L_y/R\)`.
